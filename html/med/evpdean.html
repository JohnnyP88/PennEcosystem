Innovation

Whether in research, education, or patient care, a commitment to innovation defines Penn Medicine. An expansive network of Centers and Institutes leverages talent from across the University and fuels breakthroughs in basic and translational science. We are forging new partnerships with industry to accelerate the translation of our discoveries into clinical practice and pursing novel approaches to health care delivery.
