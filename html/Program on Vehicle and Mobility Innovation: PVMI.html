Program on Vehicle and Mobility Innovation

Subscribe to Updates

The Program on Vehicle and Mobility Innovation (PVMI) is the largest and oldest international research consortium aimed at understanding the challenges facing the global automotive industry.

In 2013, the program joined the Mack Institute for Innovation Management at the Wharton School, University of Pennsylvania. Together we will focus the expertise and rigorous research methods of the PVMI global scholar network and the Mack Institute’s researchers on critical questions of mobility and the evolving automotive industry.

Contact us if you have any questions or if your organization would like to learn more about becoming a corporate partner.
