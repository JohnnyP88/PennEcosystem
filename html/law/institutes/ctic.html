Center for Technology, Innovation & Competition

CTIC delivers and promotes foundational research that aims to shape the way legislators, regulatory authorities, and scholars develop policies and regulatory frameworks for technology, intellectual property, antitrust, privacy, and related fields.

Economics of Digital Services Initiative

This joint initiative of CTIC and Penn’s Warren Center for Network & Data Sciences funds and promotes new independent research on the impact of data on the business strategies of digital platforms. Read more about the initiative and access the research, papers, and blog posts produced thus far.
