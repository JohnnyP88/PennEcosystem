Symposium on Diagnostic and Innovation Alliances at Penn Medicine
  • Print

September 12, 2022

On September 8, the Department of Pathology & Laboratory Medicine hosted the inaugural Symposium on Diagnostics and Innovation Alliances at Penn Medicine with colleagues in Pathology, Radiology, Penn Health-Tech, Penn Engineering, and from other areas across the institution to discuss their research programs on diagnostics and innovation at Penn.

Update 9/12/2022

Video of the livestream from the first Symposium on Diagnostic and Innovation Alliances is now available at this link or directly below:
